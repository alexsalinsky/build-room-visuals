## Quick orientation

- **Mission line (always verbatim):** "Learn AI. Build Robots. Stay Human."
- **What we sell:** Open Cohort (virtual 4 weeks), Closed Cohort (private/booked), Retreat (5-day in-person), Membership (alumni layer).
- **Central event:** Kickoff Session (never call it a "webinar").
- **Brand promise:** you leave with one real thing you built, the people who built it with you, and what you do next.

## How to ship in Alex's voice without checking with him every time

Three rules that get you 90% there:

1. **Short. Direct. No corporate filler.** If a sentence has "leverage," "synergies," "unlock," or any em dash, kill it.
2. **Lead with the problem, then name the missing thing, then offer the next step.** Don't lead with "we are excited to announce."
3. **Concrete over abstract.** Numbers, names, dates. Not vibes.

When in doubt, copy the rhythm from [01-tone-of-voice.md](01-tone-of-voice.md).
