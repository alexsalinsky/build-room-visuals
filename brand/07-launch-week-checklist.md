# 07 — Launch Week Checklist

Standard ship-list for the week leading into a Kickoff Session launch. Use this for any cohort launch.

---

## Timeline (anchored on the Kickoff Session)

Anchor everything on the live event. Call it T0.

| When | What |
|---|---|
| **T-2 weeks** | Pre-marketing begins. Tone-of-voice review, asset prep, calendar locked. |
| **T-2 weeks (stretch) / T-12 days (realistic)** | First IG + WhatsApp post live |
| **T-11 days** | First LinkedIn post live (Alex posts; you draft) |
| **T-2 days → T-1 day** | Scarcity push: "2 days until Kickoff Session" / "tomorrow" reminders |
| **T0** | Kickoff Session goes live |
| **T+1 week** | TSLS (Tech Setup / Ledger Session) — late buyers can still join the cohort here |

---

## Deliverables (in order)

### 1. Pre-marketing prep (T-2 weeks)
- Tone-of-voice review: read recent posts in [Reference Library](04-reference-library.md) to load Alex's cadence
- Check the [Proof Library](https://alexsalinsky.github.io/build-room-visuals/proof-library.html) for fresh quotes/wins to feature
- Confirm IG access + scheduling tool is wired
- Confirm the registration link works end-to-end

### 2. Ship the first post (T-12 to T-14 days)
- Pick one of the IG drafts from [06-starter-copy.md](06-starter-copy.md), adapt with the specific date, ship
- Image: brand-aligned background + dark text, or a real photo from Build Room asset folders
- Cross-post to Skool with the Skool variant
- WhatsApp broadcast same day or next day with the WhatsApp draft

### 3. Draft the LinkedIn post (target T-11 days)
- Send to Alex day before target post date
- He posts from his account
- If he doesn't respond by midday next, ping once

### 4. Build a simple per-launch tracker
- Columns: Date | Channel | Asset (link) | Reach | Engagement | Leads | Notes
- After the Kickoff fires, retro the launch using this

### 5. Scarcity push posts (T-2, T-1)
- "2 days until Kickoff Session" reminder, IG + Skool + WhatsApp
- "Tomorrow" reminder, day-of
- Keep these short and direct. Same voice rules apply.

### 6. Day-of-Kickoff post-mortem capture
- Note: attendees, paid registrations, conversion rate, what worked, what hurt
- Add to tracker
- Schedule the retro call with Alex within a week

---

## What NOT to do during a launch week

- ✗ Don't try to redesign the brand mid-launch. Use what exists.
- ✗ Don't post a "we're launching!" announcement. The first post is a problem statement, not an announcement.
- ✗ Don't wait too long for permission on a WhatsApp message. Ship the realistic draft; review after.
- ✗ Don't pitch a Closed Cohort offer in an Open Cohort launch. They have different paths.
- ✗ Don't over-engineer the tracker. A 7-column sheet is enough.

---

## When something goes wrong

- **You posted something and 5 minutes later you spot a typo:** edit or delete + repost. Fix in place rather than leave it.
- **Alex doesn't respond in 24h:** ship the draft. Replace later if he wants changes. Speed beats polish.
- **A post performs really badly:** capture it in the tracker with a 1-sentence "why I think it tanked" note. Learn at retro.
- **You're not sure if a quote/claim is OK to use:** check [05-featured-quotes.md](05-featured-quotes.md) for status. If still unsure, don't use it.
