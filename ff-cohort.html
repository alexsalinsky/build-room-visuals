The Cohort

What you get

  • Four sessions over 4 weeks, one per week, 2 hours each
  • Learning modules on AI fundamentals, design thinking, collaboration, and shipping your work
  • 4+ hours of Build Time with coaching and peer support
  • Coach and community support and accountability
  • Async support between sessions: ask questions, share progress, get unblocked

By the end: a working tool, a peer group who gets it, and confidence to keep building.

Don't know what to build yet? Good. That's the first thing we figure out together in Session 1.

Three Principles

Learn. Build. Stay Human.

  • Learn. Frameworks for thinking about AI, not tool memorization.
  • Build. Ship something real, with help along the way.
  • Stay Human. Community support, peer feedback, human-connection rituals.
