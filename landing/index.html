Is this you?

Be honest with yourself before applying. Most people self-select out, and that's good.

Yes, this is for you if

  • You run ops, a team, a small business, or your own practice
  • You've tried AI tools and they're sitting underused
  • You want to own AI, not "be trained on" AI
  • You'd rather ship a working thing than read another tool report
  • You can give the cohort 2 hours a week for 4 weeks

Not yet if

  • You haven't decided whether AI is for your business yet
  • You can't carve out an hour a week for 4 weeks
  • You want a course you can binge alone
  • You're looking for a flashy tech bro AI vibe (we're not that)
  • You need an enterprise procurement track — we're built for fast teams

FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What if I can't make a live session?
Sessions are recorded. The async Skool community is where a lot of the building happens between calls. Live sessions are the ignition, not the engine.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. The Build Room is for operators, not engineers. If you can use Google Sheets, you're past the technical bar.
What tools do you teach?
Claude, ChatGPT, Codex, n8n, Zapier, plus whatever your stack already uses. We pick the workflow first, then the tool. Not the other way around.
What does the guarantee actually mean?
If you don't have a working AI workflow shipped by Demo Day, I personally keep working with you free until you do. I have not had to invoke it. I'm willing to bet I won't.
When does the next cohort start?
Next webinar date is TBA. Drop your email and you'll get the invite as soon as it's locked. Cohorts kick off the week after each info session.
Can my whole team do this together?
Yes. Two options: individual seats for each teammate at $399 each, or a private team cohort customized to your workflows. Email Alex for the team option.
