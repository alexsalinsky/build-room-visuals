About

Alex Salinsky, plus a roster of coaches.

AS

Alex Salinsky runs riseCOO, an operations and leadership development practice (legal entity En Blazes LLC). He's spent the last decade helping teams turn messy ideas into running systems, most recently with AI. The Build Room is the cohort arm of that work.

He writes about AI fluency, measurement, and team adoption on his LinkedIn newsletter, hosts AI Buildathons at conferences, and runs private cohorts for teams that want to move faster than a webinar will get them. Start with How to Get Your Team to Actually Use AI.

Every cohort runs with at least one coach alongside Alex. The coach owns the accountability spine, the post-session check-ins, and keeps the group on the milestones they declared instead of the shiny objects that pull them off course. Current and past coaches include Thierry Muller (Focus Tribe).
